# Neverwinter Lockbox Master Research List

Last researched: 13 July 2026.

## Reconciliation summary

- **71 regular lockboxes** are present in the project source sheet and application database.
- **61 of 71 lockboxes** currently have at least one highlighted reward with an explicit ToonForge media mapping; **121 of 190 highlighted rewards** are mapped.
- The Neverwinter Wiki chronology names entries 1–70, from Nightmare Lockbox through Pet Pals Lockbox.
- The wiki also shows an unnamed entry dated **19 May 2026**. The project source sheet identifies that entry as **Encroaching Frost Lockbox**, with Sir Waddlelot and Snowtusk as account-unlock rewards. This name has not been independently indexed in the public sources checked.
- Four release dates conflict between the project sheet and the wiki chronology: Feywild, Magnificent Emporium, Fell Dragon, and Crushing Wave. The master research list uses the wiki dates while retaining the sheet dates in the CSV for auditability.
- The Psionic Lockbox date remains approximate: the wiki confirms it existed by 19 November 2024 but does not document an earlier announcement.
- ToonForge / Neverwinter Compendium was verified as a reward-image and item-data source, not as a standalone lockbox chronology.
- NW Hub was retained as a secondary discovery source, but its JavaScript application did not expose a publicly retrievable or search-indexed lockbox catalogue during this research pass.

## Date discrepancies found

| Lockbox | Project sheet | Internet chronology | Resolution |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Feywild Lockbox | 15 August 2013 | 22 August 2013 | Use 22 August for the regular Feywild release; 15 August aligns with the temporary Nightmare-prize period. |
| Lockbox of the Magnificent Emporium | 26 June 2014 | 27 June 2014 | Use 27 June pending a stronger primary-source timestamp. |
| Fell Dragon Lockbox | 2 November 2014 | 2 October 2014 | Use 2 October; the sheet date appears to be a month typo. |
| Crushing Wave Lockbox | 11 August 2015 | 12 August 2015 | Use 12 August pending a stronger primary-source timestamp. |

## Supplemental and non-standard boxes

These are documented as lockboxes or box-like containers, but they are not part of the normal chronological enchanted-key rotation.

1. **Magnificent Resurgence Lockbox** — Periodic post-2018 resurgence-style offering; not a normal chronological rotation entry.
2. **Astral Lockbox** — Introduced in July 2020 and periodically sold through the Wondrous Bazaar.
3. **Astral Casket** — Astral-style container with separate rewards; relationship to Astral Lockbox is not consistently documented.
4. **Tarmalune Mystery Box** — Introduced during the 2025 Summer Festival and sold by the Trade Bar Merchant.
5. **Gift of Tymora** — Tymora's Gift event container.
6. **Waukeen's Coin Purse** — Coins of Waukeen event container and exchange currency source.
7. **Gift of the Gods** — Challenge of the Gods event container.
8. **Waukeen's Strongbox** — Exchanged from Waukeen's Coin Purses; categorized as a lockbox by the wiki.
9. **Waukeen's Treasure Chest** — Exchanged from Waukeen's Coin Purses; categorized as a lockbox by the wiki.
10. **Waukeen's Golden Chest** — Exchanged from Waukeen's Coin Purses; categorized as a lockbox by the wiki.

## Source assessment

| Source | What was verified | How it is used |
|---|---|---|
| Neverwinter Wiki | Chronological table, release dates, major rewards, and links to many official announcements | Baseline chronology for the first 70 named entries and supplemental boxes |
| Official Neverwinter news | Announcement pages linked from the wiki, including recent lockboxes | Primary announcement and artwork source when a direct page is available |
| ToonForge / Neverwinter Compendium | Explicit companion, mount, and artifact image mappings; item records can identify “Lockbox” as an item source | Reward thumbnails and item cross-referencing, not the master lockbox-name list |
| NW Hub | Site identity as a Neverwinter tools/builds/combat-simulation application | Secondary discovery source; no public lockbox catalogue was independently extracted |
| Project source sheet | Complete 71-entry list and highlighted rewards | Supplies the newest Encroaching Frost entry and app-specific reward data |
